campaigns

#child abuse

protect children from dangerous parents. The Safe Child Act protects children from the abuse and trauma which causes future substance abuse and mental health issues, and substantially increases the risk of the 7 leading causes of death.

#education

Education is every child’s right. It equips them with the knowledge and skills they need to realize their potential and to protect themselves from harm. And the better quality of life education brings, translates into huge benefits for society.

#girlscan

Girls can rock! Girls can rule! Girls can do anything and everything! That is the message we want to convey charging forward ...a belief to believe in and betting it all on girls doing what they can do. This program is aimed to inspire women.

#leanintogether

We all have an important role to play in reaching equality. Lean In Together is a campaign started that encourages men to help women in the fight for gender equality. when men support women they benefit and everyone else.
